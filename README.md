Express your application as a graph of well-specified & controlled behaviors, inputs, outputs, and side effects. An AI of your choice converts the graph into working code, manages changes, and operates the app in production. Nodes can contain sub-nodes, enabling many layers of abstraction with a node representing a single unit of behavior (like a function), a component of a larger service, or even an entire micro-service. Graphs also specify permissioning, dependencies, and interfaces. Appgraphs are expressed in simple JSON, and can be version-controlled like code. Everything is local, and you can interact with appgraph using a CLI.
